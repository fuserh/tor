Tor Expert Bundle

The tor expert bundle contains the tor and pluggable transports binaries, bridge strings, and geoip data used in Tor Browser. The two versions shown indicate the version of Tor Browser these particular binaries were built for and the version of the contained tor daemon. These packages are intended for developers who need to bundle tor with their applications.
